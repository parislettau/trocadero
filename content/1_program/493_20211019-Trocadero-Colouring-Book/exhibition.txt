Text: Introducing the Trocadero Colouring Book!
-----------------------------------------

---

We've put together a very special limited edition colouring book, featuring an artist from every year in Trocadero's history.

**[SHOP HERE](https://trocaderoartspace.bigcartel.com/product/trocadero-2021-colouring-book)**

Contributing artists: Aaron Billings, Anne Kucera, Annee Miron, Anneke Wood, Cassandra Tytler, Hannah Beilharz, Janice Gobey, Jennifer Rooke, John Derrick, Katie Sfetkidis, Karyn Linder, Kathy Sarpi, Ken Wentworth, Klari Agar, Lou Molesworth, Madeleine Thornton-Smith, Merryn Trevethan, Michael Brennan, Paola Balla, Patrick Zaia, Rolando Garay-Matziaris, Sue Dodd and Tegan Iversen.
