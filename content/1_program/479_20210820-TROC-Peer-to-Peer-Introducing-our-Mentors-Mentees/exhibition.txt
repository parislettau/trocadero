![](http://www.trocaderoartspace.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/P2P_Launch_Web-1-1024x536.png)**We are excited to announce the launch of the TROC Peer-to-Peer program!**

TROC Peer-to-Peer is a Program for emerging artists to engage in peer learning, professional development, networking and skill sharing as a way to improve practice, equity and access in the arts.

Introducing our Mentors and Mentees:

We welcome Mentees **Augustine Ang, Avrille Burrows, Callum Watson, [Candy Ng](https://candyngart.com), Celine Dore, [Eme Talastas](http://www.emeeme.work) Dela Rosa, [Eric Jong](http://www.ericjong.com.au), Evangeline Clark, Fiona Knight, Indra Liusuari, Jahan Rezakhanlou, [Jas Streten](http://www.jasartist.com.au), Jason Harris, [Jessica Tanto](http://www.jessicatanto.com), [John Elcatsha](https://oushin.blogspot.com), Kelly Altair, [Mara Braun](http://braunartistmara.com), Noel Maghathe,[ Prudence Illingworth](http://prudenceillingworth.com), [Silvi Kadillari](http://www.silvikadillari.com).**

The 2021 program Mentors are[ **Azza Zein**](http://azzazein.html)**, [Caoife Power](http://www.instagram.com/caoifepower/), [Daniel Marks](https://danielrmarks.com/projects), [Louisa Bufardeci](http://www.louisabufardeci.net), [Siying Zhou](http://www.siyingzhou.com), [Youjia Lu](https://youjialu.com/home.html),[ Steven Rhall](https://stevenrhall.com) &amp; [Zara Sully](https://zarasully.com)**

TROC Peer-to-Peer is facilitated by [Corinna Berndt](https://corinnaberndt.net/) &amp; Stacy Jewell

TROC Peer-to-Peer is supported by the Victorian Government through [Creative Victoria](https://creative.vic.gov.au/).
