Text: TROC PEER-TO-PEER
=================

![](http://www.trocaderoartspace.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/IMG_4507-1024x683.jpg)**TROC Peer-to-Peer** is a new program for emerging artists to engage in peer learning, professional development, networking and skill sharing as a way to improve practice, equity and access in the arts. It prioritises artists who do not currently have access to institutional support or training.

**2021 Program Participants** [Augustine Ang](https://www.instagram.com/augustbabycollage/), [Avrille Burrows](http://www.avrilleburrows.com.au/), Callum Watson, [Candy Ng](https://candyngart.com/), [Celine Dore](https://www.instagram.com/celinedore_/?hl=en), [Eme](http://www.emeeme.work/), [Eric Jong](http://www.ericjong.com.au/), [Evangeline Clark](https://www.instagram.com/eevee.artsy/?hl=en), [Fiona Knight](https://www.watteau.org/), [Indra Liusuari](https://www.instagram.com/indraliusuari/?hl=en), [Jahan Rezakhanlou](https://www.instagram.com/haikyo.e.jahan/?hl=en), [Jas Streten](http://www.jasartist.com.au/), [Jason Harris](https://www.instagram.com/jcharris.art/?hl=en), [Jessica Tanto](http://www.jessicatanto.com/), [John Elcatsha](https://oushin.blogspot.com/), [Kelly Altair](https://www.instagram.com/kaltair.art/?hl=en), [Mara Braun](http://braunartistmara.com/), [Noel Maghathe](https://www.instagram.com/intheshowersorry/),[ Prudence Illingworth](http://prudenceillingworth.com/), [Silvi Kadillari](http://www.silvikadillari.com/).
