ABOUT

Trocadero Art Space is an artist-run initiative presenting a diverse and experimental program of exhibitions and events. A non-profit art space run by volunteers, Trocadero strives to provide a safe and engaging environment for artists to exhibit in, develop from, and contribute to the Maribyrnong community.

PEOPLE

Trocadero Art Space is run entirely by a dedicated team of volunteers. Our committee and gallery sitters are artists, arts workers, and people passionate about the arts.

Outside the box
01.07.2022

Outside the Box | Conversations

Outside the Box is a four-part conversation series that sets out to explore and celebrate cultural shifts influenced by diverse creators in Australia. 

Presenter & Special Program Curator Wintana Kidane will take the audience on an intimate journey through the stories of inspirational black and Indigenous creators, artists and businesspeople.

This series is a unique opportunity to get to know local creators and connect with the local community. Together, we hold a space for storytelling and conversations that both inspire and challenge our perspectives. These conversations are about identity, ownership, representation and building sustainable communities.

Wintana Kidane is an African Australian podcaster, presenter and the co-creator of the Bittersweet Podcast. She is Trocadero's Special Programs Curator.

Outside the Box | Conversations #1: Creating Your Own Narrative

Panelists: **Sabina McKenna, Sky Thomas and Mariam Koslay

Outside the Box | Conversations #2: Finding Your Voice

Panel 2 explores creativity, self-expression and identity from the perspectives of Black creators, and discuss how they navigate and pave their own path in creative industries.

Panelists: Jesse daCosta, Titan Debirioun and Tig Terera
