ABOUT

Trocadero Art Space is an artist-run initiative presenting a diverse and experimental program of exhibitions and events. A non-profit art space run by volunteers, Trocadero strives to provide a safe and engaging environment for artists to exhibit in, develop from, and contribute to the Maribyrnong community.

PEOPLE

Trocadero Art Space is run entirely by a dedicated team of volunteers. Our committee and gallery sitters are artists, arts workers, and people passionate about the arts.

COVIDSafe Information

Trocadero’s focus is to provide a COVIDSafe environment for our artists, visitors and the broader community.

Ashen Shadows explores the transformative potential of the processes of grief and mourning within the context of rapid climate change. It examines our position and shifting identity in relation to a disappearing natural world and the emotional effects of ecological loss, both in collective political denial and individual nostalgic yearning for an idealised time in the past.
